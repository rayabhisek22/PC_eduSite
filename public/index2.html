With the same motto Pragati classes was started. We have our vision and objectives like -

  • To Empower students to build stronger concepts and to make them more confident and energetic to face any academic challenges in the future.
  • To give quality education so that students not only excel in Boards but also in various competitive exams like NEET, JEE Main and JEE Advanced.
  • To deliver the best study materials which would be sufficient for Boards and competitive exams.
  • To help the students in realizing their true potential.
  • To use modern online platforms for tests and teaching so that the students can feel safe and relaxed while giving competitive exams.
  • To have a concrete doubt clearing sessions so that one will always stay ahead and could enhance their knowledge.
